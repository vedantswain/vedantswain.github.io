Hello There...

I'm a Human-Computer Interaction enthusiast pursuing my PhD in Computer Science at Georgia Tech. I'm passionate about technology driven solutions to problems of human wellbeing. Unlike traditional notions of HCI, my work focusses on the invisible interactions with ubiquitous devices that can explain social dynamics in communities.
