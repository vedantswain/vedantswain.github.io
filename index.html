    Prior Work and Experience

I study approaches to repurpose every day technologies to unobtrusively support productivity and wellbeing. I aim to identify responsible and human-centered ways to deploy such frameworks in the workforce to inform personal insights, personnel management, data-driven policy design, and the future of work.
