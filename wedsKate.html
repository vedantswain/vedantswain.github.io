Venue

Our wedding ceremony and reception will take place at The Wimbish House. This is a historic mansion located in midtown. Although originally owned by the Wimbish family, it has been the historic meeting place for the Atlanta Women's Club for over 100 years. For our wedding, we wanted to find a nice cozy spot that everyone can comfortably reach.
