Hi!

I'm Vedant Das Swain, a PhD student in Computer Science, specializing in Human-Computer Interaction. I am co-advised by Prof. Gregory D. Abowd and Prof. Munmun De Choudhury. I recently completed my MS in HCI from Georgia Tech, where I continue my doctoral studies as well. I plan on continuing research in this field and build, as well as explore, innovative user-centric solutions for grounded but pervasive issues.

My interests are primarily at the intersection of ubiquitous computing and social computing. I like to extend beyond simply context-aware systems and try to understand socially-aware systems, that can help researchers understand how humans behave in communities. In my work I use opportunistic behavior sensing through unobtrusive mobile data collection to analyse human behavior and in turn inform the design of interventions that work towards improving personal health and wellbeing.
